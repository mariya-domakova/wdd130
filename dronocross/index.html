Welcome to our Dronocross website

We are a drone cervices company based in Ireland. Our purpose is to bring technology closer to people to make their lives easier and happier. We focus on providing search and rescue services by using our highly equipped drones. Along with that, we help in wind turbine inspections and make spectacular photos and videos for various occasions.

Why get our help

Efficient

Save time and life of your loved ones by using our drones to provide real-time information on the location of missing persons in a quick and safe methods.

Professional

Our skilled pilots are highly trained and skilled in flying drones in challenging environments and under difficult conditions such as complex terrain, mountainous regions or dense forests. Excellent communication skills and ability to work effectively under pressure allows them to make decisions that can have a significant impact on the success of the mission.

Superhuman

Our drones equipped with thermal cameras and infrared sensors detecting heat signatures difficult to spot with the naked eye from a distance, which helps rescue missing persons quicker and drastically increases the chances of their rescueing.
