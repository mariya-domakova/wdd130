Overview

Purpose

This website is created to offer a great opportunity for those who seek extreme adventures. Rafting is one of the most popular outdoor activities around the world and a way to have a memorable experience. Our website helps discover spectacular rafting destinations in the US and provides an easy interface for booking tours.

Audience

Ages 18 - 60; outdoor enthusiasts with good physical training; people whose travel destinations are Siberia and Central Asia; families, friends and couples who want to have an unforgettable vacation; individuals who want to experiance something new.
