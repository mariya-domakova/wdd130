About Me

Mariya Domakova

Hi! I am a 26 years old girl living in Moscow, Russia. Here are some facts about me:

I am convinced that it's never late to change occupation! After pursuing my first Bachelor's degree in teaching German and English, I realized I had chosen the wrong path. During the pandemic I first got an idea of changing my profession and taking some programming courses. That was the time I was introduced to BYU Pathway, a program that changed my life. When I was in the third semester of PathwayConnect, I took a python class with no programming background, and I loved it! Now I want to learn other programming languages to become a web developer.

I am a proud member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I got baptized in 2018 at the age of 21. In October 2022 I was finally able to attend a temple for the first time and get my own endowment.

I love travelling and exploring cultures! I have visited 7 countries so far. My goal is to work remotely, so I can go abroad any time I want. Having started my degree in programming is the first step towards my dream!
